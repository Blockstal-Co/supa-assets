## Tips for Email Use

1. **Recommended sizes**: 24x24, 32x32, or 40x40 pixels
2. **Always include alt text** for accessibility
3. **Test across email clients** - Some may require PNG fallbacks
4. **Keep spacing consistent** between icons
5. **Link to your actual profiles** - Update the href URLs
6. **Choose the right version**:
   - **Color**: Best for brand recognition
   - **Black**: Better compatibility with some email clients, works on light backgrounds
   - **White**: For dark backgrounds or themed email templates

## Brand Guidelines

These icons use official brand colors (color versions). Always check individual platform brand guidelines before use in commercial applications.
